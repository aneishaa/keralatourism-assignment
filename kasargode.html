Welcome to Kasaragod

This northernmost district of Kerala is famous for its coir and handloom industries and is also home to some exquisite folk arts and rituals of Kerala

Mahalingeswara Temple

Famous for its ancient Siva Temple belonged to have been founded by Arjuna. The place where the Kiratha Yuddham battle between Arjuna and Lord Siva took place according to legend. The adjacent forest not frequented by men is considered the abode of Lord Siva and his divine consort.
